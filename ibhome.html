Where and how to start web development?
Get yourself familiar with basics of html,css and build few static sites until you get good grasp on the fundamentals
0.Understand what is web d(front-end+back-end stuff) get overall picture of it.
1.w3schools.com for learning.[Has really solid programs]
2.Want hands-on learning?do freecodecamp certification(this one is the best)-prefer this after learning basics on w3schools.
3.Once you get familiarity with html,css then start learning js basics on same w3.
4.Watch as much as tutorials on youtube(don't go in tutorial hell)learn everything by doing.
5.Learn touch-typing.
6.Use IDE's such as vscode,sublime text,atom etc.For webd vscode is prefered alot.
7.Don't rush into learning frameworks,libraries at the initial stages.
Fundamentals will fetch more benefits in long term.

What is the general selection process for a job?
Generally there are 3 types of companies

Small range companies
   -Aptitude
   -Group Discussion
   -Technical interview
   -HR interview

Mid range companies
   -Aptitude & technical MCQ's
   -Medium level coding round
   -Technical interview-1
   -Technical interview-2
   -HR interview

High range companies
   -Technical MCQ's
   -Multiple coding rounds
   -Hackathon
   -Multiple technical interview
